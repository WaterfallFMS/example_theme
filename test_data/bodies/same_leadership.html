Post Leadership

Big Sky Post consist of military and civilian engineers from Malmstrom AFB and civilian engineers from the City of Great Falls, MT. Members of the post meet to discuss current events as well as to visit sites with new and innovative engineering works.

Post Officers

Post President, Sally Riker

Mrs. Riker has a depth of experience in both public and private sectors. Sally is a graduate of the University of Georgia and is active in several organizations including SAME, ULI, WTS, ASHE, CREW, ACEC, G4BT,SASHTO, and many others.

Sally Riker was recently named to "40 Under 40 - Georgia's Best and Brightest"
